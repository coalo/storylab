# Chapter Commission

chapter_id: "001"
commission_revision: 1
status: commissioned

## Story-purpose context

Shift material dependence into personal leverage.

## Reader-facing promise

An intimate contest between livelihood, pride, and reluctant trust.

## POV boundary

Lin Yue knows the shop is short of rice but not who bought the last cart.

## Immediate lived state

She is cold, hungry, and opening before the market bell.

## Immediate desire

Obtain enough grain for the noon trade without borrowing.

## Counterforce desire

The mill owner wants an old debt acknowledged before releasing stock.

## Dramatic pressure

Waiting loses the market; accepting his terms exposes a weakness.

## Canon facts

- The shop holds twelve copper coins.

## Required state change

The grain problem and their balance of leverage are both different by the end.

## Protected information

- Keep the earlier buyer's identity unknown.

## Creative freedom

The author owns action, dialogue, pacing, imagery, discovery, silence, and the route to change.

## Optional private possibilities

The editor may imagine a barter, but it is not an instruction and must not be exported.
